Drop background-music files in THIS folder to use them in ClipStudio.

- Supported: .mp3, .m4a, .wav, .aac, .ogg, .flac
- After adding a file, it shows up in the "Background music" dropdown under
  Advanced options when you create a project (refresh the page if it's open).
- The track is looped quietly UNDER the clip's audio (about 18% volume), so
  speech stays clearly on top.
- Use music you have the rights to (royalty-free / your own). ClipStudio does
  not ship any music.
